AFRICA WLI WATERFALLS.
LOCATION: Wli waterfalls is located 20km from Hohoe in the Volta Region of Ghana. This waterfall is surrounded by some amazing wildlife when one takes a walk through their wildlife sanctuary. History. The Wli waterfalls is the highest water fall in West Africa located in Ghana. The Falls is known locally as Agoomatsa waterfalls meaning, “Allow Me to Flow”. It is situated approximately 280 kilometres from Accra the capital city of Ghana.
